**Sydney Harbour Dioxin Calculator**

A simple, intuitive web application designed to help you easily track and manage your dioxin intake from consuming seafood caught in Sydney Harbour (east of the Harbour Bridge). All calculations strictly adhere to the official guidelines from the NSW Food Authority, ensuring accurate and reliable data.

- **Intuitive Dashboard**: Visually displays your monthly dioxin intake percentage at a glance, keeping you informed of your health status in real-time.
